+ The Short

My name is Chelsea Jan, and I'm a photographer based in sunny San Diego, CA. My photography primarily ranges from product, portraiture, to landscape. Some of my steadily-brewed passions include front-end development, photography, rock climbing, road cycling, and yoga.

+ The Long

I'm originally from the Bay Area, which means I was destined to be an engineer. With big aspirations to apply modern engineering design to healthcare, an industry that could really improve efficiency in clinical spaces by advancing existing outdated technology, I pursued my B.S. in Bioengineering (Electrical Engineering track) from the University of California, San Diego, where I published my first research publication in cardiovascular image processing, raced competitively in collegiate road cycling, and drank too many cups of coffee to count. My favorite subjects from school were in Digital Signal Processing, Medical Instrumentation, Non-linear Modeling, and surprisingly – Acting. Yes, I'm a huge nerd.

For 40 hours/week, I'm a Software Engineer within Product Development at Illumina, where I develop E2E workflows on customer-facing desktop applications used to operate, generate, and analyze bioinformatics data from the sequencers. I'm also currently working on a Front-End Development Certification course through UC San Diego Extension.

Nothing is more satifying to me than anticipating and capturing the perfect shot. Because of photography, I've traveled to new places, saved important memories, met other inspiring photographers, and learned to better understand human emotion. I am currently working on, what will be, my first self-published landscape photography book.
